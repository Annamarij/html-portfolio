The Best Movies According to Anna

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Atomic Falafel

A funny Israeli movie that highlights the israeli-palestinian issue from the point of view of a falafel maker and her teenage daughter.

Addams Family

A classic for the goth at heart, and a warm-hearted reminder to embrace your weirdness.

Fried Green Tomatoes

A lovely depiction of the rural American South and bonds that last through generations.
